## 🏛️ Sovereignty Manifesto
**Is the GreenProof Protocol Sovereign?**

Yes. Sovereignty in GreenProof is defined by **four pillars**:
1. **Agentic Autonomy**: Decisions are made by a decentralized quórum of agents (Physical, Juridical, Ethical) in the CRE, independent of any central entity.
2. **ZK-Privacy**: The asset owner maintains sovereignty over their raw data; the protocol only exports a "True/False" proof of compliance.
3. **Mathematical Proof**: Trust is not based on reputation but on Groth16 Snark proofs verified on-chain.
4. **Institutional Portability**: Via CCIP, the "Truth" generated by GreenProof can travel across blockchains without losing its sovereign integrity.
